## Assessment plan

A 12-question quiz, grouped into four sections matching the lesson files (Bloom Basics & Troubleshooting; Simple Books & Translation; Primers, Decodable & Leveled Readers; Audio & Interactive Publications), with an 80% pass threshold (10/12), covers procedural recall and tool/template-selection judgment for objectives 1, 2, 3, 5, 6, 7, 9, and 10. The 4-scenario bank, sequenced foundational-to-complex (simple book creation/translation → formatting → decodable-to-leveled reader → talking book), is where hands-on software performance is actually verified and graded holistically against the mentor guide's rubric, covering objectives 3, 4, 5, 7, 8, 9, and 10 — since a multiple-choice quiz can't confirm a learner actually operated the software, every "perform/configure" objective gets at least one scenario in addition to any quiz coverage. Objectives 4 and 8 (translation and leveled-reader configuration) are scenario-only, as they are best judged by inspecting the learner's output rather than by recall questions.
