# Stage 6 — Internal review

**Board status:** `Internal Review` · **Who:** Internal Reviewer (**not** the Author) · **Tool:** PR review

A final human read for pedagogy, tone, and learner-readiness before the course is piloted.
This is **not** a re-run of the alignment or SME checks — trust those; look at the things a
machine and a fact-checker can't.

## Entry criteria

- Alignment check passed ([stage 4](04-alignment.md)) and SME fact-check passed
  ([stage 5](05-sme-factcheck.md)), with both recorded on the tracker issue.
- Move the board status to `Internal Review`.

## How

Review the course's PR. Read as if you were the learner:

- Does it teach, or just inform? Does each lesson set up the task, teach the minimum, and
  hand off to practice?
- Is the tone right for adult professional learners?
- Is anything confusing, out of order, or assuming knowledge a learner at this outcome
  level won't have?
- Are the scenario bank and mentor guide usable by a real facilitator?

Leave comments; when it's ready, **approve and merge the PR**. That merge is the exit
signal for this stage.

## Exit criteria

- The course PR is merged to `main`.

## Then

- ✅ Tick **"6. Internal review passed"** on the tracker issue.
- Go to [Stage 7 — Pilot](07-pilot.md).
