# Working in a Multicultural Environment

**Category:** Core

## Sub-competencies

### Intercultural Communication

Communicates with people from other cultures in a respectful and appropriate manner.

**Observable criteria**

- Identifies different communication styles and adapts accordingly.
- Recognizes that leadership or conflict management can vary by culture.
- Remains flexible and open-minded in intercultural training and mentoring.

**Why it matters**

- Good intercultural communication fosters collaboration and prevents misunderstandings that could hinder teamwork.

### Multicultural Work

Works effectively in teams or projects involving people of diverse cultural backgrounds.

**Observable criteria**

- Considers cultural differences when encouraging positive group dynamics.
- Actively participates in decision-making by including all voices.
- Respects local authorities and customs.

**Why it matters**

- Understanding and valuing cultural differences contribute to a harmonious work environment and better project outcomes.
- **Technical problem-solving**
- **Interpersonal and intercultural competencies**
- **Adult education** and **mentoring**
- **Data organization, communication, and management**
