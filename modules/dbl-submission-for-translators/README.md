# DBL submission for translators

**DBL Submission Form**TO BE SUBMITTED ONLY ONCE FOR EACH PROJECT. 
After the first submission (as little as one book), please do the following: Correct the Project Canon to include ALL books that are ready to be digitally published; Do a Send/Receive; Notify dbladmin_intl@sil.org that the text needs to be updated.
This form must be completed in order for a Wycliffe/SIL stewarded text to be added to the Digital Bible Library (DBL). Once this form has been submitted, it is understood that the text may then be uploaded to the DBL and licensed according to the selections marked on this form by the person responsible for the submission. 
**Please read down through the fields below before beginning to complete this form so that you are prepared with all the information needed.** This form has 27 questions. Your email address is automatically added, and as such is not visibly numbered.
If you are unable to answer all the questions, you can close the form and re-open it later when you have more information. Google will automatically save your responses as a draft for up to 30 days. Simply open the form again (using the same URL from the same Google account) and continue filling in the form. When you are finished you can submit the form.
Once your form has been submitted, you have another 30 day window where you are allowed to change or add to your responses. In order to change a response, find the "acknowledgement of receipt" email sent to you by Google Forms. It will have a button that takes you to your original responses, and allows you to change them.
