# Language Use

**Category:** Core · **Guide section:** Language and Culture

## Sub-competencies

### Social Anthropology

Respects cultural differences and knows how to work according to local norms.

**Observable criteria**

- Observes and learns about the customs and practices of the community.
- Interacts with empathy and respect for cultural differences.
- Adapts communication style to local cultural standards.

**Why it matters**

- A sound cultural understanding helps ensure that communities accept the consultant’s work and remain engaged with the tools and processes.

### Use of Language

Communicates clearly in the most appropriate vehicular language.

**Observable criteria**

- Expresses ideas clearly and understandably, both verbally and in writing.
- Chooses the right language for the context to ensure comprehension by all.
- Simplifies technical concepts when explaining to non-specialists.

**Why it matters**

- Adequate proficiency in the local or vehicular language is crucial for training and assisting teams without communication barriers.
