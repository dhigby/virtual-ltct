# Professional Networking

**Category:** Core · **Guide section:** Relationships with Other Organizations

## Sub-competencies

### Influencing and Building a Network

Maintains respectful and constructive relationships with external partners, either to learn from them or to have a positive influence.

**Observable criteria**

- Clearly communicates the project’s values and needs in the LT field.
- Represents local linguistic communities by highlighting their challenges and priorities.
- Seeks potential partners or useful contacts.

**Why it matters**

- Developing a network and advocating for project needs help secure support and foster productive collaborations.

### Relationship with Partners

Collaborates effectively with a range of partners and represents one’s own organization professionally.

**Observable criteria**

- Explains the importance of language technology to non-specialists.
- Respects the principles and values of other organizations.
- Aims for mutually beneficial (win-win) solutions.

**Why it matters**

- Positive partnerships can lead to new funding opportunities, technical support, and greater recognition of the work being done.
