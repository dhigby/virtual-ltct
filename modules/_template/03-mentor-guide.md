# Mentor guide

**Purpose:** Guide for mentors assessing learner responses to the scenario bank.
Matches the pattern in `modules/coretech-computer-hardware/05-mentor-guide.md`.

## What you're evaluating

1. **Reasoning** — do they think systematically or jump to conclusions?
2. **Technical/domain knowledge** — do they understand the underlying concepts correctly?
3. **Practical judgment** — are recommendations appropriate given the scenario's constraints?
4. **Communication** — are explanations clear and actionable?

## Providing feedback

**Strong responses:** acknowledge what they did well; point out excellent reasoning;
suggest minor refinements.

**Adequate responses:** confirm what was correct; highlight what was missed; ask probing
questions to develop their thinking.

**Weak responses:** identify where the reasoning went wrong; work through the scenario
together; assign additional practice in the specific area of weakness.
