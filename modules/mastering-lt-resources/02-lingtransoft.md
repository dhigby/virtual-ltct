# Mastering the LingTranSoft Website

**Estimated time:** 45 minutes

**Purpose:** To learn how to use the [lingtransoft.info](http://lingtransoft.info/) website in LT work.

## Learning objectives

- You will be able to find information on language and translation software on the [lingtransoft.info](http://lingtransoft.info/) website.
- You will know how to use the LT Competencies List on the [lingtransoft.info](http://lingtransoft.info/) website to develop a competency.

## Connect

**Why should you use the [lingtransoft.info](http://lingtransoft.info/) website?**

You should use lingtransoft.info because it serves as a specialized, searchable archive for anyone working in language and translation work. Lingtransoft.info is the go-to resource if you need to discover language and translation software, understand what skills are needed at your level, or get guidance on accomplishing specific language-related tasks.

## Content

LingTranSoft is a specialized website designed to help users identify language and translation software and explore professional competencies. The platform features a task help section that breaks down specific goals, such as literacy development, and a software database that filters programs by language support and operating system.

Furthermore, the site hosts a competency list detailing the skills required for different proficiency levels, ranging from learners to experts. While the site is a valuable archive for software discovery, it differs from the [lingtran.net](http://lingtran.net/) website, which focuses more on instructional tutorials and consultant training. Users are encouraged to utilize the news tab for the most recent updates, as the website lacks an automated notification system for new entries.

- [lingtransoft.info](http://lingtransoft.info/) is a directory designed to help you discover programs, understand what they support, and identify the competencies you need — not to host tutorials or training materials.

**Question:** LingTranSoft is described as two distinct platforms serving two different purposes. What are these platforms, what role does each play in a language practitioner's workflow, and how do they complement each other?

- LingTranSoft operates through two separate platforms designed for different stages of a practitioner's work. lingtransoft.info serves the *discovery phase*, offering directories, task guides, and competency lists that help practitioners identify what tools exist and what tasks they support. lingtran.net, on the other hand, serves the *application phase*, providing tutorials, teaching guides, and training materials that help practitioners actually learn and use those tools.
- Together, they mirror the broader four-step workflow the platform promotes: defining the work, equipping the project, mastering the craft, and tracking the ecosystem. A practitioner might begin on lingtransoft.info to filter tools by task and constraint — for example, finding Bloom by searching for a primer development tool with a French UI requirement — and then move to lingtran.net to build the hands-on skills needed to use that tool effectively. Neither platform is sufficient alone; the discovery platform loses value without the training resources to follow through, and the training platform is most useful when a practitioner already knows which tools are relevant to their specific domain and tasks.

## Challenge

Go to the [lingtransoft.info](http://lingtransoft.info/) website.

- Identify a software you are interested in. Review the information you find on it. Write down three pieces of information about it.
- Click one of the other competencies that you have an interest in. Then review growth activities, relevant tasks, and further information for that competency. Then determine the next steps you would take to further your development in that competency.
