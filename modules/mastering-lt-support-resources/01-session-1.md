# Session 1: Introduction to LT Support Websites
**Duration:** 60 minutes
**Level:** Has Knowledge
**Goal:** Navigate and effectively use lingtran.net, lingtransoft.info, and LT News to find training materials, software information, and current language technology topics
---
## Connect
**Time:** 10 minutes
Think about a time when you needed to help someone with a language technology problem or train someone on an LT tool. Where did you look for information? How confident were you that you found the best resources?
As Language Technology consultants and support staff, we often find ourselves in situations where we need to quickly locate training materials, understand new software, or stay current with developments in our field. The challenge isn't that resources don't exist—it's knowing where to find them efficiently.
### Reflection Questions
Consider your current approach to finding LT resources:
- When someone asks you about a piece of software, where do you typically look first?
- How do you currently stay informed about new developments in language technology?
- What frustrations have you encountered when trying to find training materials for others?
This session introduces three foundational websites that will become essential tools in your daily work. Mastering these resources directly supports three competencies in your CBC growth plan: problem solving, giving responsive feedback to developers and website maintainers, and providing adult education to others.

## Content
**Time:** 30 minutes
### Section 1: Lingtran.net — Your Training Hub
Lingtran.net serves as the primary repository for language technology training materials. This site is maintained specifically for our community and contains curated content designed for training contexts.
**What you'll find on lingtran.net:**
- Structured training plans organized by topic
- Downloadable webinar recordings
- Training videos for specific software tools
- Materials designed for both self-study and group instruction
The site organizes content topically, making it straightforward to locate materials when preparing for a training event. When you need to train someone on a specific tool or concept, lingtran.net should be your first stop.
**Practical tip:** When preparing for a training event, search for existing training plans before creating your own. Many topics already have tested curricula that you can adapt to your context.
### Section 2: Lingtransoft.info — Software and Competency Development
Lingtransoft.info functions as a comprehensive directory of language and translation software, combined with professional development resources through the LT Competencies framework.
**Software Information**
For each piece of software, you'll typically find:
- Overview and purpose descriptions
- System requirements and compatibility information
- Links to documentation and support resources
- Related tools and alternatives
This makes lingtransoft.info invaluable when responding to questions about software capabilities or when helping someone choose the right tool for their situation.
**LT Competencies List**
The competencies section provides a structured approach to professional development. For each competency area, you'll find:
- Growth activities to build your skills
- Relevant tasks that demonstrate competence
- Further information and resources for deeper learning
**Example:**
If you're developing expertise in Scripture App Builder, you would find the competency entry, review the suggested growth activities, identify relevant tasks you could undertake, and access links to further training—all in one place.
### Section 3: LT News — Staying Current
LT News, hosted on Notion, keeps you informed about developments across language technology. Unlike the structured resources on lingtran.net and lingtransoft.info, LT News provides timely articles on current topics, updates, and emerging tools.
**Access:** https://tinyurl.com/35m9599r
LT News serves a different purpose than lingtran.net. While lingtran.net provides structured training materials, LT News offers ongoing information about what's happening in the field right now. Think of it as staying current with the conversation in our community.
The Notion platform provides powerful search capabilities, allowing you to find articles on specific topics of interest and follow links to related resources.
### Key Takeaways
- **Lingtran.net** is your primary source for training materials, webinars, and training videos—go here first when preparing to train others
- **Lingtransoft.info** provides comprehensive software information plus the LT Competencies framework for your professional development
- **LT News** keeps you current with developments and serves as a searchable archive of articles on LT topics
- These three resources together support your problem solving, training delivery, and ability to give informed feedback

## Challenge
**Time:** 15 minutes
### Activity: Exploring the Three Websites
**Objective:** Gain hands-on familiarity with each website by completing focused tasks that mirror real-world use cases.
**Instructions:**
Complete the following tasks, taking brief notes on what you discover:
**Task 1: Lingtran.net (5 minutes)**
1. Think of a topic you might need to train someone on in the next few months
2. Navigate to lingtran.net and search for a training plan related to that topic
3. Evaluate what you find: Would this training plan work for your context? What adaptations might you need?
4. Locate the webinar section and find one recording that interests you
**Task 2: Lingtransoft.info (5 minutes)**
1. Choose a piece of software you work with regularly or want to learn more about
2. Find its entry on lingtransoft.info and note two pieces of useful information you discover
3. Navigate to the LT Competencies section and find a competency relevant to your current development goals
4. Review the growth activities, relevant tasks, and further information for that competency
5. Identify one next step you could take for your development
**Task 3: LT News (5 minutes)**
1. Search LT News for a topic of your choice
2. Visit one article and follow at least one relevant link
3. Consider: How does the information in LT News differ from what you found on lingtran.net?
**Questions to Consider:**
- Which website do you think you'll use most frequently in your current role?
- How might these resources change your approach to answering LT questions from others?
- What did you find that surprised you or that you wish you'd known about earlier?

## Change
**Time:** 5 minutes
### Applying What You've Learned
These three websites become more valuable with regular use. Rather than treating them as occasional references, consider integrating them into your routine workflow.
**For problem solving:** When someone brings you an LT question, start by checking lingtransoft.info for software information and lingtran.net for training materials before searching more broadly.
**For training preparation:** Always check lingtran.net for existing training plans and videos before developing new materials from scratch.
**For staying informed:** Set aside regular time to browse LT News so you're aware of developments that might affect the people you support.
### Next Steps
- **Before the next session:** Show someone else how to use these three websites. Teaching others is one of the best ways to solidify your own understanding.
- **In your daily work:** When you encounter an LT question this week, consciously try using these resources as your first point of reference.
- **Note your questions:** As you use these sites, write down any questions or challenges you encounter. Bring these to our next session for discussion.
### Looking Ahead
In Session 2, we'll continue exploring additional language technology support resources that complement what we've learned today. Come prepared with questions about lingtran.net, lingtransoft.info, and LT News from your experience using them this week.

## For Mentors
### Assessment Guidance
**Learner demonstrates understanding when they:**
- Can navigate to each of the three websites without assistance
- Can articulate the distinct purpose of each site (training materials vs. software information vs. current news)
- Successfully locates relevant information for a given scenario (finding a training plan, software details, or news article)
- Can explain when they would use each resource in their work context
**Common misconceptions to watch for:**
- Confusing lingtran.net with lingtransoft.info—reinforce that lingtran.net focuses on training while lingtransoft.info focuses on software information and competencies
- Treating LT News as a training resource rather than a current awareness tool—help learners understand the different purposes these resources serve
- Expecting to find everything in one location—emphasize that each site has distinct strengths and using them together provides comprehensive coverage
### Differentiation Suggestions
**For struggling learners:**
- Pair them with a more experienced colleague during the Challenge activities
- Provide specific search terms to start with rather than asking them to choose their own topics
- Follow up individually between sessions to check their comfort level with navigation
**For advanced learners:**
- Challenge them to identify content gaps on any of the sites and consider how they might contribute
- Ask them to prepare a brief demonstration of an advanced feature for the group
- Encourage them to mentor others during the home assignment of teaching someone else to use the sites
