# MENTOR GUIDE - Part 1
**Purpose:** Guide for mentors assessing learner responses to scenarios **Format:** Detailed answer keys and assessment criteria
## How to Use This Guide
### Assessment Principles
**What You're Evaluating:**
1. **Diagnostic Reasoning** - Do they think systematically or jump to conclusions?
2. **Technical Knowledge** - Do they understand hardware components and failure patterns?
3. **Practical Judgment** - Are recommendations appropriate for the context?
4. **Communication** - Are explanations clear and actionable?
5. **Prevention Mindset** - Do they think beyond the immediate fix?
**Important Notes:**
- Look for sound reasoning more than "perfect" answers
- There are often multiple valid approaches
- Assess the process, not just the conclusion
- Use scenarios to identify learning needs
### Providing Feedback
**For Strong Responses:**
- Acknowledge what they did well
- Point out excellent reasoning or insights
- Suggest minor refinements or additional considerations
**For Adequate Responses:**
- Confirm what was correct
- Highlight what they missed or could improve
- Ask probing questions to develop their thinking
- Point them to resources or concepts to study
**For Weak Responses:**
- Identify where reasoning went wrong
- Work through the scenario together
- Focus on teaching the diagnostic process
- Assign additional practice in areas of weakness
